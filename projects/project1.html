Enterprise Wireless Deployment

I architected and deployed a secure, high‑performance wireless infrastructure for a mid‑sized company. This project required building the environment from scratch, integrating enterprise‑grade authentication, traffic segmentation, and advanced policy enforcement to deliver reliability, scalability, and strong security.

Topology Diagram

Goals

  • Deliver seamless Wi‑Fi coverage across offices, meeting rooms, and common areas.
  • Implement strong authentication and granular access control using RADIUS.
  • Isolate guest traffic from corporate resources through VLAN segmentation and firewall rules.
  • Prioritize critical business applications and VIP subnets using QoS and policy routing.
  • Provide visibility and optimization through heatmap surveys and usage analytics.

Approach & Tools

  • Network Architecture: Multi‑AP environment with VLAN segmentation, policy routing, and centralized management.
  • Authentication: RADIUS server integration with PPSK for user‑specific credentials and role‑based access.
  • Traffic Management: QoS rules prioritizing VIP subnets and mission‑critical applications, plus time schedules, range limits, and session limits.
  • Guest Access: Guest portals with bandwidth controls, VLAN isolation, and firewall enforcement.
  • Monitoring & Analytics: Heatmap surveys to optimize AP placement, visualize coverage, and track usage trends.
  • Tools Used: Omada Controller, RADIUS server, VLANs, firewalls, enterprise APs, centralized dashboards.

Challenges & Solutions

  • Coverage Gaps: Solved with detailed heatmap surveys and AP repositioning.
  • Guest Security: Enforced VLAN isolation, strict firewall rules, and bandwidth limits.
  • Performance Bottlenecks: Mitigated by prioritizing VIP subnets and critical applications through QoS and policy routing.

Benefits

  • Reliable Wi‑Fi coverage with minimized dead zones.
  • Enhanced security via RADIUS authentication, PPSK, and guest segmentation.
  • Improved performance by prioritizing critical applications and VIP subnets.
  • Actionable insights via heatmaps and analytics for ongoing optimization.
  • Future‑proofed design supporting scalability and additional AP deployments.
